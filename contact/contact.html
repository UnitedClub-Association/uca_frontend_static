Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join a club?

To join a club, you need to register through our Join UCA page. Once registered, you can browse available clubs and submit your application to join.

What are the membership fees?

Membership fees vary by club. Basic UCA membership is free for all students. Some specialized clubs may have additional fees to cover materials and activities.

How can I start a new club?

To start a new club, you need to submit a proposal to the UCA executive committee. The proposal should include the club's purpose, activities, and potential membership. Contact us for more details on the application process.

When do clubs typically meet?

Club meeting schedules vary. Most clubs meet weekly or bi-weekly, typically after school hours or during designated activity periods. Check each club's page for specific meeting times and locations.

Can I join multiple clubs?

Yes, you can join multiple clubs based on your interests and availability. We encourage students to explore different activities, but be mindful of time commitments to ensure you can actively participate in each club.
