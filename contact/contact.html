Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join a club?

To join a club, you need to register through our Join UCA page. Once registered, you can browse available clubs and submit your application to join.

What are the membership fees?

Membership fees vary by club. Basic UCA membership is free for all students. Some specialized clubs may have additional fees to cover materials and activities.

How can I start a new club?

To start a new club, you need to submit a proposal to the UCA committee. The proposal should include the club's purpose, activities, and a list of at least 10 interested members. Contact us for more details.

When do clubs typically meet?

Most clubs meet weekly or bi-weekly after school hours. Some clubs also organize weekend activities. Each club sets its own schedule based on members' availability.
