Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join a club?

To join a club, obtain the official UCA registration form from a club executive. The membership fee is 20 Taka per club, and you can join up to 3 clubs.

What are the membership fees?

The membership fee is 20 Taka per club, with a maximum of 3 clubs. For those applying for an executive position, a 50 Taka registration fee is required.

How can I start a new club?

Submit a proposal to the UCA executive committee, detailing the club’s purpose, planned activities, and potential membership. Contact us for the application process.

When do clubs typically meet?

Most clubs meet weekly or bi-weekly, typically after school or during activity periods. Visit individual club pages for specific schedules and locations.

Can I join multiple clubs?

Yes, you can join up to 3 clubs, depending on your availability. Ensure you can balance your commitments to participate actively in each club.
