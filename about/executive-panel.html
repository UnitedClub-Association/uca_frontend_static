UCA Executive Panel

Meet the dynamic leaders steering the United Club Association. Our Executive Panel comprises the dedicated presidents of each club, working collaboratively to foster a vibrant, inclusive, and engaging campus culture at our institution.

Current Council (2024-2025)

Our inaugural council is a collective of visionary founding club presidents. They are the architects of UCA's collaborative spirit, laying a robust foundation for an enriching club ecosystem designed to empower and benefit students for years to come.

UCA Governance Framework

  • Digital & Tech Initiatives: Spearheaded by the ICT Club President, driving UCA's online presence and tech infrastructure.
  • Campus Engagement & Activities: A collaborative leadership effort by all Club Presidents to ensure diverse and vibrant events.
  • Strategic & Administrative Oversight: Guided by the UCA President (if applicable, or a collective body) to ensure smooth operations and inter-club synergy.
