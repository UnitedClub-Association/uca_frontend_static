Future Councils

The UCA has established a sustainable leadership transition model to ensure continuity and innovation. Each new council builds upon the foundation laid by its predecessors while bringing fresh perspectives to our growing community.

Oct 10, 2024 - Dec 31, 2025

Inaugural Council

Our founding executives are establishing UCA's organizational framework, creating sustainable processes, and launching signature initiatives that will define our association's identity.

  • Establishment of digital infrastructure and online presence
  • Development of inter-club collaboration framework
  • Launch of inaugural cross-club events and initiatives
Jan 1, 2026 - Dec 31, 2026

Second Council

The second generation of leadership will expand UCA's reach and impact, strengthening existing programs while introducing innovative approaches to student engagement.

  • Expansion of club membership and diversity
  • Enhancement of signature events based on first-year feedback
  • Development of alumni engagement initiatives
Jan 1, 2027 - Dec 31, 2027

Third Council

Building on a solid foundation, the third council will focus on sustainability and broader impact, establishing UCA as an integral part of university culture and student life.

  • Implementation of long-term sustainability measures
  • Expansion of community outreach and social impact initiatives
  • Development of leadership training programs for future councils
