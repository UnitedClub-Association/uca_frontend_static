Current Council (2024-2025)

Our inaugural council consists of the founding club presidents who collaborate to lead the United Club Association. The current term runs from October 10, 2024 to December 31, 2025.

UCA Structure

  • Online Activities: Led by ICT Club President
  • Offline Activities: Collaborative effort by all Club Presidents
  • Council Term: Current term ends December 31, 2025
  • Future Terms: January 1 to December 31 annually

Future Councils

Starting January 1, 2026, new councils will be formed annually with presidents from each club. Elections for club presidents take place at the end of each year.

Oct 10, 2024 - Dec 31, 2025

Inaugural Council

Founding executives establishing UCA's foundation and initial club activities.

Jan 1, 2026 - Dec 31, 2026

Second Council

New leadership continuing to build on the established foundation.

Jan 1, 2027 - Dec 31, 2027

Third Council

Further expanding UCA's reach and impact across the university.
