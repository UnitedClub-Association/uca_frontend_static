About UCA

United Club Association: Established October 10, 2024

Our Mission

The United Club Association (UCA) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of clubs at University Laboratory School and College. We aim to empower students by providing resources, guidance, and a platform for collaboration among clubs.

Through our efforts, we strive to create an environment where students can explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and contribute to the school's culture.

Our Core Values

Community

We believe in building a strong sense of community among students, faculty, and staff through collaborative events and initiatives.

Excellence

We encourage excellence in all activities and initiatives undertaken by our clubs, supporting high standards in every endeavor.

Inclusivity

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all students, regardless of background or interests.

Innovation

We foster innovation and creative thinking, encouraging clubs to explore new ideas and approaches to student activities.

Our Structure

The UCA is led by the presidents of all member clubs, with the ICT Club President serving as the lead for online activities. This collaborative leadership model ensures that all clubs have equal representation and voice in the association's decisions.
