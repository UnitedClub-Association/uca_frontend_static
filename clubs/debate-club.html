About Our Club

The UCA Debate Club fosters critical thinking, public speaking, and persuasive argumentation skills. We empower students to engage in meaningful discourse on contemporary issues, develop logical reasoning, and become confident communicators.

As the first club to join UCA in October 2024, we've set the foundation for excellence in student leadership and intellectual discourse.

  • Competitive Debating
  • Public Speaking
  • Critical Analysis
  • Leadership Development
