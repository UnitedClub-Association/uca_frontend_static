About Our Club

The Debate Club provides a platform for students to develop critical thinking skills and build confidence in public speaking. We focus on learning the fundamentals of argumentation and respectful discourse.

Our members practice various debate formats and discuss contemporary issues in a supportive environment. Whether you're experienced or just starting out, we welcome all students interested in improving their communication skills.

  • Bi-weekly meetings on Mondays at 6:30 PM in the Student Union
  • Learn different debate formats and techniques
  • Practice in a supportive, constructive environment
  • Participate in inter-university debate competitions
