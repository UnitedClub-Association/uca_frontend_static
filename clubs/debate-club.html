About Our Club

The Debate Club provides a platform for students to develop critical thinking skills and build confidence in public speaking. We focus on learning the fundamentals of argumentation, persuasive techniques, and respectful discourse in a supportive environment.

Our members practice various debate formats and discuss contemporary issues that matter. Whether you're experienced or just starting out, we welcome all students interested in improving their communication skills and expanding their perspectives.

  • Weekly practice sessions with experienced mentors
  • Learn different debate formats and persuasive techniques
  • Practice in a supportive, constructive environment
  • Participate in inter-university debate competitions
  • Public speaking workshops and guest lectures

Join The Conversation

Ready to develop your voice and challenge your thinking? Join our vibrant community of debaters and critical thinkers. We meet every Tuesday and Thursday in the Student Center, Room 305.
