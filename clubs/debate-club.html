About Our Club

The University Laboratory Debate Club is a dynamic community dedicated to the art of persuasive speaking and logical reasoning. We provide a platform for students to develop critical thinking skills, build confidence in public speaking, and engage with contemporary issues through structured debates.

Our members participate in various debate formats including Parliamentary, Lincoln-Douglas, and Model UN, gaining valuable experience that extends beyond the classroom. Whether you're a seasoned debater or just curious about the activity, we welcome all students interested in honing their argumentative skills.
