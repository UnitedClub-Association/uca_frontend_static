About Our Club

The Literature Club is a sanctuary for lovers of the written word. We explore the rich tapestry of world literature, from classical works to contemporary masterpieces, fostering appreciation for diverse literary traditions and nurturing creative writing talents.

As the seventh club to join UCA in October 2024, we've established ourselves as a vital cultural cornerstone, promoting literary appreciation and creative expression in our university community.

  • Literary Discussions
  • Creative Writing Workshops
  • Poetry Recitations
  • Literary Competitions
