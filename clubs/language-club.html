About Our Club

The Language Club is dedicated to promoting linguistic diversity and cultural understanding through language learning. We focus on developing communication skills in various languages while exploring the cultures associated with them.

Founded in February 2024, we are the ninth club to join UCA. While we operated independently for the first six months, we continue to foster language learning and cross-cultural communication in our university community.

  • Conversation Practice
  • Language Exchange
  • Cultural Movie Screenings
  • International Language Days
