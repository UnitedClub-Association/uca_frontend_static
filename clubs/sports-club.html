About Our Club

The Sports Club promotes physical fitness, sportsmanship, and competitive excellence among students. Our mission is to foster athletic development, team spirit, and healthy competition through a variety of sporting activities.

As the third club to join UCA in October 2024, we've established a strong foundation for athletic excellence in our university community.

What We Offer

  • Regular training sessions and matches
  • Professional coaching and mentoring
  • Inter-college tournaments
  • Fitness and conditioning programs
  • Annual sports festival
  • Scholarship opportunities
