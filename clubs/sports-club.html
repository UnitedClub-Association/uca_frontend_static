About Our Club

Welcome to the Sports Club of University Laboratory School and College. We bring together passionate athletes to create an energetic community of sports enthusiasts under the United Club Association. Our mission is to promote physical fitness, sportsmanship, and competitive excellence among students.

  • Regular training sessions and matches
  • Professional coaching and mentoring
  • Inter-college tournaments
  • Fitness and conditioning programs
  • Annual sports festival
  • Scholarship opportunities

Join Our Team

Be part of our dynamic sports community. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, there's a place for you in our club. Follow us for updates on matches, training sessions, and upcoming tournaments.
