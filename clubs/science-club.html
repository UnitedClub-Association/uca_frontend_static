About Our Club

The Science Club is dedicated to exploring the wonders of scientific discovery through hands-on experimentation, research, and innovation. We foster a spirit of scientific inquiry and provide opportunities for students to engage with cutting-edge STEM concepts.

As the fifth club to join UCA in October 2024, we've become a catalyst for scientific exploration and innovation in our university community.

  • Advanced Equipment Access
  • Research Mentorship
  • Peer Collaboration
  • Scientific Competitions
  • STEM Workshops
