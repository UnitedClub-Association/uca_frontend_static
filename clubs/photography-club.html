About The Club

The Photography Club is a community of creative minds dedicated to exploring the art and science of photography. We are a space to learn, share, and grow together through visual storytelling.

From beginners learning composition to advanced members mastering complex lighting, our goal is to elevate the craft for everyone involved.

  • Composition & Framing Workshops
  • Mastering Light & Shadow
  • Post-Processing & Editing Techniques
  • Curated Photo Walks & Exhibitions
