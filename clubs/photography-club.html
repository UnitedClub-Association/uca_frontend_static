About Us

The Photography Club is where passion meets perspective. We're a community of creative minds dedicated to exploring the art and science of photography. From beginners to advanced photographers, our club provides a space to learn, share, and grow together through visual storytelling.

As the second club to join UCA in October 2024, we've been instrumental in documenting and showcasing the vibrant life of our university community.

  • Photography skills development
  • Monthly workshops and technique tutorials
  • Equipment sharing and learning opportunities
  • Photo exhibitions and competitions
  • Collaborative projects with other clubs

Join Us

Interested in capturing moments and perspectives? All clubs are part of the United Club Association (UCA). Visit the main UCA page to learn how to become a member and get involved!
