About Us

The ICT Club is at the forefront of digital innovation and technological advancement in our university. We bridge the gap between theory and practical application, providing hands-on experience in programming, web development, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

As the sixth club to join UCA in October 2024, we've taken charge of the association's digital infrastructure and online presence, showcasing the transformative power of technology in student organizations.
