Snapfest 2024

November 17, 2024 | TSC Auditorium, University of Dhaka

Snapfest 2024, originally launched as "Freeze the Moment," was an exhilarating photography contest hosted by the University Laboratory Photography Club. Students captured the essence of the award ceremony, with Ishrak, founder of Noksha Visuals, judging the finest shots.
