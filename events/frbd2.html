About The Event

Frame of Revolution: Bangladesh 2.0

Frame of Revolution: Bangladesh 2.0 is a collaborative visual storytelling initiative that captures the spirit, struggle, and environmental impact of the July 2024 student movement. Through photography, art, and video, this event seeks to spotlight youth resistance and raise awareness about the ecological consequences of state repression.

Main Segments

Mobile Photography

Theme: Anything related to the July protest. Capture raw, unfiltered moments that reflect the intensity, emotion, and unity of the movement—right from your mobile device.

DSLR Photography

Theme: Anything related to the July protest. Use your lens to deliver powerful, artistic interpretations of protest scenes, student solidarity, and the evolving revolution.

Story Writing: Screams Without Voices

Theme: Protest graffiti is more than paint on walls—it’s the unheard scream of a movement. Write a micro-story (under 1000 words) imagining the untold tale behind real graffiti from the July 2024 protests.

Special Segments

Poster Design

Theme: "Tear Gas in the Air, Resistance on the Ground". Design a striking poster that explores how the use of tear gas and other militarized tools contributes to environmental degradation.

Video Content

Theme: "Toxic Repression: A Threat to Both Life and Nature". Create a short video that illustrates how state-enforced violence harms both students and the environment.

Event Dates: 7 July to 35 July 2025
