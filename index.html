United Club Association

With the heaviest of hearts, we acknowledge the devastating tragedy that occurred at Milestone School and College. Our deepest condolences go out to the families who have lost loved ones, and our thoughts are with all those injured and affected by this heartbreaking event.

In a period of mourning and out of respect for the victims and their families, the United Club Association website and all online activities will be suspended on July 21st and 22nd, 2025.

We will resume our usual activities on July 23rd, 2025. We thank you for your understanding during this time of profound grief.

- The Executive Panel
